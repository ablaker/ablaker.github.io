Extracurriculars

Model UN, Girl Up, Pen Pals

Model UN is a club that has recently returned to Taft and I am so glad to be a part of it. The club is a team of around 15 students. We meet biweekly to discuss current events and to further learn rules of procedure and the workings of a model UN conference. I had participated in a virtual conference the year before coming to Taft but had never experienced the true, in-person, experience. My first (real) conference was a success! Model UN has taught me public speaking skills as well as a better sense and understanding of the evolving world we live in.

I also am part of a club at Taft called Girl Up. It is an all-female group of about 10 girls. We meet weekly to discuss problems and predjudices against women locally and internationally. Although Taft demonstrates and prides itself in equality, there are still issues regarding feminism and the treatment of women that stem from the foundation our school was built upon.
